let, N = 1000


Constant:       O(1)        -> 1
Linear:         O(N)        -> 1000
Logarithmic:    O(logN)     -> 10
Square root:    O(sqrt(N))  -> 31.62
Quadratic:      O(N*N)      -> 1000000
Linearithmic:   O(Nlog(N))  -> 10000


Constant > Logarithmic > Square root > Linear > Linearithmic > Quadratic
